# Career Performance Analysis: Valtteri Bottas (2012-2024)

**OVERVIEW**
This Project analyzes the Formula 1 career of Valterri Bottas from 2012 to 2024, focusing on performance, consistency and race outcomes.
The dashboard hgihlights key career metrics such as total wins, podium finishes, race retirements, and performance trends over time.

**OBJECTIVES**
- Evaluate overall career performance metrics
- Analyze win and podium trends over time
- Identify top-performing circuits
- Examine causes of race retirements (DNFs)
- Compare starting and finishing positions

- **KEY INSIGHTS**
- Valterri Bottas maintained strong consistency with 67 podium finishes across his career
- Peak performance occured during his tenure at Mercedes, resulting in 10 career wins
- A total of 26 DNFs highlights reliability challenges across dirfferent seasons
- Certain circuits consistently yielded strong results, indicating track-specific strengths
- Starting position has a noticeable impact on final race outcomes, though position gains occur in competitive races

  - Power Query ( Data Cleaning)
  - DAX ( Measures & KPIS)
